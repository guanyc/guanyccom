# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: June 2026**

Thank you for using **Take a Break**.

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it.

## Information We Collect

Take a Break does not require account registration and does not directly collect personal information such as:

* Name
* Email address
* Phone number
* Physical address
* Contacts
* Photos
* Location

The app is designed to work without creating a user account.

## Purchases

If you choose to purchase premium content or features, payments are processed securely by Google Play through the Google Play Billing system.

We do not receive or store your payment card information.

For information about how Google handles payment data, please review Google’s Privacy Policy.

## Customer Support

If you contact us for support by email, we may receive information you voluntarily provide, such as:

* Your email address
* Information included in your support request

This information is used only to respond to your inquiry and provide support.

## Children’s Privacy

Take a Break may be used by children.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

The app does not require users to create accounts, provide personal information, or submit content.

If a parent or guardian believes that a child has provided personal information through support communications, they may contact us and we will delete the information when reasonably possible.

Take a Break does not use advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, behavioral profiling, personalized advertising, or cross-app tracking technologies.

## Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party service:

* Google Play Billing (for processing purchases)

These services may collect information as described in their own privacy policies.

## Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information received through customer support communications.

However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
