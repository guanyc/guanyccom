## Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** [March 1, 2026]

**HanziNova** ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

### a. Personal Information

We do **not** directly collect personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, or phone number.

---

### b. Usage Data

We may collect anonymized data about how you interact with the app, including:

* App usage events
* Feature interactions
* Session duration
* Device type and OS version

This data is collected via:

* Firebase Analytics

---

### c. Crash Data

If the app crashes, we collect diagnostic information such as:

* Device model
* OS version
* Crash logs

This helps us fix bugs and improve performance.

Collected via:

* Firebase Crashlytics

---

### d. Advertising Data

We use third-party advertising services that may collect and use data to show relevant ads.

* Google AdMob may collect:

  * Advertising ID
  * Device information
  * Interaction with ads

---

### e. Configuration Data

We may remotely adjust app behavior and features without requiring updates using:

* Firebase Remote Config

This may include:

* Feature flags
* UI variations
* Experiment settings

---

## 2. How We Use Information

We use collected data to:

* Improve app performance and stability
* Understand user behavior and improve features
* Deliver and optimize advertisements
* Customize app experience

---

## 3. Third-Party Services

We rely on trusted third-party services:

* Google (Firebase, AdMob)

These services may collect data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

You can review:

* [https://policies.google.com/privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

---

## 4. Data Sharing

We do **not** sell your personal data.

We may share data only:

* With service providers (e.g., Google Firebase, AdMob)
* When required by law

---

## 5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data, but no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

---

## 6. Your Choices

You can:

* Reset your advertising ID in device settings
* Opt out of personalized ads (depending on your region)

For Android:

* Settings → Privacy → Ads

---

## 7. Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed to children under 13.
We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

---

## 8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.
Changes will be reflected with a new "Effective date".
