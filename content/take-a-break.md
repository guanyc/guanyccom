## How it works

Simply choose how you’re feeling, and Take a Break gives you a short, personal spiritual pause:

- A carefully selected KJV Bible verse
- A short, practical spiritual insight
- A simple, heartfelt prayer
- Optional audio playback
- Easy copy & share tools

## Why people love it

- Emotion-based devotionals: Scripture that meets you where you are
- A quick spiritual reset: no pressure, no long sessions, no catching up
- Gentle encouragement for real life: peace, strength, comfort, guidance
- Audio + sharing: listen or send encouragement in seconds
- Minimal & distraction-free: focus on reflection, not features

## FAQ

**Is this a full Bible app?**  
Take a Break is a lightweight devotional experience—built for moments when you only have a minute. It’s designed to help you pause, breathe, and reconnect with Scripture.

**Which Bible translation does it use?**  
King James Version (KJV).

**Do I need an account?**  
No. Take a Break is designed to be simple and private.

**Does the app collect personal data?**  
Take a Break does not require an account and does not directly collect personal information such as your name, phone number, or address. The app does use Firebase Analytics, Firebase Crashlytics, and Google AdMob, which may collect limited technical and usage data such as app interactions, device information, advertising identifiers, crash logs, diagnostics, and ad-related data so we can improve app stability, understand usage, and support the app with ads. If you make a purchase, payment processing is handled securely by Google Play.

For more, see the Take a Break privacy policy: https://www.guanyc.com/take-a-break/privacy/
